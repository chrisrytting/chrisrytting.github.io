# Privacy Policy — Learning Loop

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

## Overview

Learning Loop ("the App") is built and maintained by Chris Rytting. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

Learning Loop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App runs entirely on your device.

Specifically:
- **No account required** to use the App
- **No analytics or tracking** tools are included
- **No data is sent** to any server
- **No cookies** are used
- **No personally identifiable information** is collected

## Third-Party Services

The current version of Learning Loop does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Future Updates

Future versions of the App may introduce optional features that involve data collection (such as user accounts for saving preferences). If and when this occurs, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and any data collection will require your explicit consent.

## Children's Privacy

Learning Loop does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no features that require personal information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

## Disclaimer

Learning Loop is a self-help tool designed to support emotional awareness. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified mental health professional or call the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
